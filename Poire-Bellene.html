Poire-Bellene

Home

Poire-Bellene is a traditional French dessert made with poached pears and a sweet wine sauce. It is a simple and elegant dish that is perfect for entertaining or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe pears
  • 1 bottle (750ml) red wine
  • 1 cup (200g) sugar
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 vanilla bean, split
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 1 orange, zested
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) water
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) Grand Marnier or other orange liqueur

Steps

  1. Peel the pears, leaving the stems intact. In a large saucepan, combine the wine, sugar, cinnamon stick, vanilla bean, lemon zest, orange zest, and water. Bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. Add the pears to the saucepan and simmer for about 20-30 minutes, or until the pears are tender. Remove the pears from the saucepan and set aside.
  3. Continue to simmer the wine sauce until it is reduced by half. Remove the cinnamon stick and vanilla bean, then stir in the cream and Grand Marnier.
  4. Place each pear in a serving dish and spoon the wine sauce over the top. Serve warm or chilled, and enjoy!
